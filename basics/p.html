Empowering Women in Technology

Welcome to Tram Technologies, a community dedicated to empowering women in the field of technology. We believe in the power of women in tech and aim to provide resources, support, and networking opportunities for women passionate about ICT and technology.

About Us

Tram Technologies was founded by Martha Nassimba, a passionate advocate for women in technology. With years of experience in the industry, Martha recognized the need for a supportive community where women could thrive and excel in tech-related fields.

Our mission is to bridge the gender gap in technology by providing mentorship programs, workshops, and networking events tailored to the needs of women. We believe that by empowering women in tech, we can create a more diverse and innovative future.
